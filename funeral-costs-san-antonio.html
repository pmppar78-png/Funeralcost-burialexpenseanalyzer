Your Rights in San Antonio

FTC Funeral Rule Protection Every funeral home in San Antonio must provide an itemized General Price List, let you choose only the services you want, and accept caskets purchased elsewhere. You are never required to buy a package. Read the full FTC Funeral Rule guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a funeral cost in San Antonio?

The average traditional funeral in San Antonio costs approximately $7,560, which is close to the Texas state average of $7,200. Direct cremation starts around $1,785.

Is cremation or burial more common in San Antonio?

In the San Antonio area, the cremation rate follows Texas's overall rate of 48%. Cremation remains the more affordable option, with direct cremation costing $1,785 compared to traditional burial at $7,560.

How do San Antonio funeral costs compare to the national average?

San Antonio funeral costs are below the national average of $7,848 for a traditional funeral. The San Antonio metro area's cost of living somewhat influences local funeral pricing.

Where can I compare funeral home prices in San Antonio?

Under the FTC Funeral Rule, every funeral home in San Antonio must provide a General Price List. Call 2-3 providers to request their GPL. You can also visit Parting.com or the Funeral Consumers Alliance for price comparison resources.
