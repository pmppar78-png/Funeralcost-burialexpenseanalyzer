Your Rights in Jacksonville

FTC Funeral Rule Protection Every funeral home in Jacksonville must provide an itemized General Price List, let you choose only the services you want, and accept caskets purchased elsewhere. You are never required to buy a package. Read the full FTC Funeral Rule guide.

Ways to Reduce Costs in Jacksonville

  • Compare prices from at least 3 funeral providers in the Jacksonville area

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a funeral cost in Jacksonville?

The average traditional funeral in Jacksonville costs approximately $8,190, which is close to the Florida state average of $7,800. Direct cremation starts around $1,890.

Is cremation or burial more common in Jacksonville?

In the Jacksonville area, the cremation rate follows Florida's overall rate of 62%. Cremation remains the more affordable option, with direct cremation costing $1,890 compared to traditional burial at $8,190.

How do Jacksonville funeral costs compare to the national average?

Jacksonville funeral costs are above the national average of $7,848 for a traditional funeral. The Jacksonville metro area's cost of living somewhat influences local funeral pricing.

Where can I compare funeral home prices in Jacksonville?

Under the FTC Funeral Rule, every funeral home in Jacksonville must provide a General Price List. Call 2-3 providers to request their GPL. You can also visit Parting.com or the Funeral Consumers Alliance for price comparison resources.
