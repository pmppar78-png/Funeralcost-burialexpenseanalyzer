Privacy Policy

We believe people navigating loss deserve complete privacy. We built this site to be as private as possible. This policy explains, in plain language, what information we collect and what we do not.

Information we do not collect

We designed this site with privacy as a priority. We understand that our visitors are often going through one of the most difficult times of their lives, and they deserve to research sensitive topics without being tracked, profiled, or marketed to.

  • No personal data collection. We do not ask for or store your name, address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information through normal use of this site.
  • No tracking cookies. We do not set cookies to track your browsing behavior, build advertising profiles, or follow you across the web.
  • No analytics tracking. As of the date below, we do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking platform. If we add privacy-respecting analytics in the future, we will update this policy and note the change.
  • No email harvesting. We do not collect email addresses through the site. We do not have a mailing list or newsletter sign-up.
  • No user accounts. There are no accounts, logins, or registration on this site.

AI chat privacy

Our AI helper allows you to have a private conversation about funeral costs, options, and questions to ask providers. Here is how that works:

  • Messages are processed by OpenAI. When you use the AI helper, your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy governs how they handle that data.
  • We do not store your conversations. Your chat messages are not saved, logged, or recorded by us. We have no database of user conversations.
  • Conversations are not saved between sessions. If you close your browser or navigate away, the conversation is gone. There is no chat history feature.
  • Do not share sensitive personal information. While we do not store your messages, we recommend not sharing Social Security numbers, financial account details, or other sensitive personal information in the chat as a general precaution.

Contact correspondence

If you choose to email us (for example, to share your experience, report a concern, or suggest a correction), we will receive and retain that correspondence only as long as needed to respond to your message and improve our content. We do not share, sell, or use your email correspondence for marketing purposes. We do not add you to any mailing list.

Third-party services

This site uses a small number of third-party services to function. Here is what each one does and what data they may receive:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to load the Inter and Merriweather typefaces. When your browser requests these fonts, Google receives your IP address and basic browser information (user agent string). Google's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy. We chose Google Fonts for reliability and performance, but want you to know this connection exists.

Netlify hosting

This site is hosted on Netlify. Like all web hosting providers, Netlify maintains basic server logs that may include IP addresses, request timestamps, and pages visited. These logs are used for security and operational purposes. Netlify's handling of this data is governed by their privacy policy. We do not have access to individual visitor log entries.

OpenAI API

The AI helper feature sends your chat messages to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI may retain API inputs and outputs in accordance with their privacy policy and terms of use. We encourage you to review their policies if you have concerns about how your messages may be handled on their end.

Cookies

We do not set tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any first-party cookies for analytics purposes. Third-party services (such as Google Fonts or Netlify) may set essential or functional cookies as part of their normal operation. These are not under our control, and we do not use them to identify or track you.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The content on this site is intended for adults who are making funeral and burial decisions or researching costs on behalf of their families.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and revise the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically. If we ever introduce analytics, advertising, or any form of data collection, we will clearly disclose it here before doing so.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at:

privacy@funeralcostanalyzer.com
