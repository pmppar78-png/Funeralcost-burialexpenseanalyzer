Usage Guidelines

We want as many people as possible to have access to accurate funeral cost data. Here are the guidelines for using the widget on your site:

  • Free to use with attribution — Both widgets are completely free. The only requirement is that the attribution link at the bottom of the widget remains intact and links back to our site.
  • Do not modify the attribution link — The link to Funeral Cost & Burial Expense Analyzer must remain visible and functional. This ensures readers can access the full data source.
  • Data source — All figures are from the 2026 National Funeral Cost Index, compiled from NFDA survey data and publicly available consumer research.
  • Do not alter the cost data — The figures in the widget reflect verified national averages. Please do not change the numbers, as this could mislead readers.
  • Blogs and news sites — The widget is ideal for articles about personal finance, end-of-life planning, consumer rights, and cost-of-living analysis.
  • Financial planning sites — Financial advisors and planners may embed the widget on educational pages or client-facing resources.
  • Educational resources — Schools, libraries, nonprofits, and consumer advocacy organizations are welcome to use the widget.
  • Commercial funeral homes — Funeral homes and cremation providers may embed the widget on their own websites to provide national context alongside their local pricing.
  • Width adjustments — You may change the max-width value to fit your page layout. The widget is responsive and will adapt to its container.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the funeral cost widget free to use?

Yes, the widget is completely free. You may embed it on any website — blogs, news sites, financial planning sites, educational resources, and even commercial funeral home websites — at no cost. The only requirement is that you keep the attribution link intact so readers can access the full data source.

Can I modify the widget design or data?

You may adjust the max-width to fit your layout, but please do not alter the cost data, remove the attribution link, or change the source URL. The data is maintained by Funeral Cost & Burial Expense Analyzer and is based on NFDA survey data and publicly available consumer research. Altering the figures could mislead your readers.

How often is the widget data updated?

The data in the embed code reflects our 2026 National Funeral Cost Index. We update our cost data annually when new NFDA and industry survey results are published. If you embed the widget, we recommend bookmarking this page and checking back periodically for updated embed code with the latest figures.

Do I need permission to use the widget?

No special permission is required. By providing this embed code publicly, we grant you a free, non-exclusive license to display the widget on your website as long as the attribution link remains intact and the data is not altered. You do not need to contact us before embedding the widget.
